About Me

Welcome to my photography portfolio! I'm Alex Morgan, a passionate photographer with over 8 years of experience capturing special moments and turning them into lasting memories.

My journey began when I received my first camera as a birthday gift. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a lifelong passion. I specialize in portrait, landscape, and event photography, with a keen eye for detail and composition.

I believe that every picture tells a story, and my goal is to tell yours in the most beautiful way possible. Whether it's a wedding, a family portrait, or a breathtaking landscape, I strive to capture the essence and emotion of the moment.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking in nature, exploring new places, or teaching photography workshops to aspiring photographers.
